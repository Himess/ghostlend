### P4 — Fresh-account transfer behavior (ERC7984ZeroBalance vs clamp-to-0)

Throwaway wallet **Y** = `0x23F2969487eAF6a48227F63D9DDc5CDAC7Cda16C` (never held cUSDC), pk (audit) = `0x63142bb4a666110f22ce61fe1d97ea74e0b23f2112f7691ce8cdf055887e4515`.
Funded with 0.08 ETH for gas: `0x1c80de0ab7deb31d7e2db9bf7af7cee4aa9850d6a0d45c2656ce7e5fc66550d8`.
Token = cUSDC wrapper `0x7c5BF43B851c1dff1a4feE8dB225b87f2C223639`.

**Zero-balance attempt** — Y calls `confidentialTransfer(main, enc(1), proof)` with an empty, never-initialized balance:

> **REVERT** — selector `0x5ff91cdc` = **`ERC7984ZeroBalance(0x23F2…a16C)`** (verified: `keccak256("ERC7984ZeroBalance(address)")[:4] == 0x5ff91cdc`; arg = Y's address). raw data=`0x5ff91cdc…c7cda16c`

**Funded retry** — Y minted+wrapped 0.1 USDC, then transfers 1 unit to main:

> SUCCESS tx=0x4766b8cc1ff07c0dcd1bdc70e16491f3731cfb5e47cf4a99644416582d4f398c status=1 gasUsed=448172

**RESULT: REVERTS (does NOT clamp).** The deployed cUSDC wrapper keeps the `ERC7984ZeroBalance` guard — matching Zama's ConfidentialWrapper docs and **contradicting the OZ v0.5.1 library** (which removed the revert and clamps to 0). Resolves pack UNCERTAIN #14/#2.

**Decision forced:** any path that moves tokens FROM a possibly-never-funded account (e.g. a liquidation seize, or a `confidentialTransferFrom` on a user who set an operator but has an empty balance) MUST `try/catch` or pre-check, because it reverts rather than clamping. Normal deposits are safe (the depositor holds a balance). Do not assume the OZ clamp-to-0 semantics on the real mock.
